Our Preventative Services

We focus on maintaining your natural teeth through rigorous cleaning and maintenance protocols.

Scale & Polish

Our signature service. Complete removal of plaque and tartar buildup followed by a professional polish for a smooth finish.

Best for: Adults & Seniors

Fluoride & Sealants

High-concentration fluoride varnish application and fissure sealants to protect children's teeth from decay.

Best for: Children & Teens

Cosmetic Stain Removal

Specialized air-polishing to remove stubborn stains caused by tea, coffee, and tobacco use.

Best for: Coffee/Tea Drinkers
